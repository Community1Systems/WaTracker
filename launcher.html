WaTracker

Record

WaTracker hooks into the WhatsApp web app to repeatedly check if your friends are online. This information is collected and stored locally on your computer so you can know research how how people use WhatsApp.

Visualize

First of all, while recording you can see in real time who is using WhatsApp at the moment.

The information collected by WaTracker is of type: "Dan came online at 17:14" or "Rachel went offline at 03:00". This information can we displayed in beautiful "timeline" graphs. You can find out how many time people spend using WhatsApp. If they use it a lot you will also know when they go to sleep and when they wake up. If two of your friends were texting for an hour at 3AM, you will know that too!
